Screensaver

When enabled, the TV idles into the slideshow (and returns to it automatically after a video ends). When disabled, the TV idles into a solid yellow placeholder. The Linux console is never shown either way. The screensaver never wakes the TV on its own.

The cache auto-rotates every day at 05:00 (keeps a random 25% of yesterday's images, replaces the other 75% with Reddit's current top posts). Press Rotate now to run it on demand.
